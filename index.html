Spaces designed to provoke emotion, challenge habit, and elevate the human interaction.

JEAME’s design process heavily thrives on exploration, beginning with immense amounts of instinct-based sketching and experimental concept developments, that are later refined through rigorous problem-solving processes. The designs are deeply inspired by cinematic and theatrical scenography, as spaces are most interesting when furniture act as physical extensions of architecture, and the people a direct extension of the spaces, and through this belief, spaces we inhabit become the strongest pillars of human experience.

We design to provoke emotion, using physical environments to convey characters, stories, and people.
